# Input Examples

## Messy Facilitator Notes Transformed to Clean Summaries

**Messy Notes:**  
- uh, we discussed, like, uh, the budget for the next, um, quarter and, um, it was kind of, like, all over the place. 
    
**Clean Summary:**  
- Discussed budget allocations for the next quarter; need to streamline priorities for better organization.

## Transcript Snippets to Structured Output

**Transcript Snippet:**  
- Participant A: "I think we should allocate more resources to marketing."
- Participant B: "But we also need to consider the R&D budget."
    
**Structured Output:**  
- **Participant A:** Proposes increased resources for marketing.  
- **Participant B:** Highlights importance of R&D budget consideration.

## Minimal Notes with Flags for Missing Information

**Notes:**  
- Meeting Date: [2026-03-23]  
- Present: [Person 1, Person 2]  
- Agenda Items:  
    - Budget Discussion [Missing Details: Priorities, Allocations]  
    - Project Updates [Missing Details: Specific Updates]  

Flags indicate areas where further information is needed.
